    Will this play when my phone screen is off?

    On a desktop browser or when you switch to another tab, audio keeps playing — that works perfectly.

    On a phone with the screen locked, YouTube itself blocks embedded players from playing in the background (it's reserved for YouTube Premium). To get as close as possible on mobile:

    • Tap 🔒 Lock to block accidental taps — the music keeps playing and you hold the button to unlock, so you can't bump a button and stop the song.
    • Tap ⛶ Fullscreen for a distraction-free full-screen player.
    • Use the lock-screen media controls (play / pause / next) that this app registers.
