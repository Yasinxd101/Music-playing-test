About background playback (read me)
  • Desktop / another tab: audio keeps playing when you switch tabs or minimize the window. ✅
  • Phone, screen locked: mobile browsers and YouTube intentionally pause embedded video when the screen is off. Lock-screen play/pause controls will appear here via the Media Session API where your browser supports it, but uninterrupted locked-screen playback of YouTube is officially only available with YouTube Premium.
  • Tip: tap Install app (or "Add to Home Screen") for the best background behavior your device allows.
